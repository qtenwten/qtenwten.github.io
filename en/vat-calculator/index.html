Free VAT Calculator Online

Add VAT, remove VAT, or calculate tax in seconds

Helpful Guide

Free VAT calculator for prices, invoices, and quotes

Use this online VAT calculator to add VAT to a net price, remove VAT from a gross amount, or calculate the tax portion separately. It works well for quotes, invoices, bookkeeping, and everyday pricing checks.

A practical VAT tool for accountants, freelancers, ecommerce sellers, agencies, and anyone who needs a quick tax calculation without spreadsheets.

What the calculator can do

  • Add VAT to a net amount instantly
  • Extract VAT from a gross amount
  • See the tax amount separately
  • Use common VAT rates: 5%, 10%, 18%, 19%, 20%, and 22%
  • Get updated results as soon as values change
  • Work with fast, clear, and accurate totals

How to use the VAT calculator

  1. Choose calculation type: add VAT, remove VAT, or calculate tax
  2. Enter the amount
  3. Select VAT rate
  4. Get instant results including net price, gross price, and VAT amount

Example calculations

Add VAT: Add VAT: if your net price is 10,000 and the VAT rate is 20%, the total becomes 12,000 and VAT is 2,000.

Remove VAT: Remove VAT: if 12,000 already includes 20% VAT, the calculator shows a net amount of 10,000 and VAT of 2,000.

Calculate VAT: Calculate VAT only: for 10,000 at 20%, the tax amount is 2,000.

FAQ

How do I remove VAT from a total amount?

Choose the remove VAT option, enter the total amount, and the calculator will show the net amount and the VAT portion.

How do I add VAT to a price?

Choose add VAT, enter the base amount, and the calculator will instantly show the price including tax.

Which VAT rates does this tool support?

The calculator supports 5%, 10%, 18%, 19%, 20%, and 22% VAT rates.

Can I use this VAT calculator for invoices and quotes?

Yes. It is designed for quick pricing checks, invoices, proposals, and day-to-day bookkeeping tasks.
