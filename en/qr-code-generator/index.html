FAQ

How do I make a QR code online for free?

Choose the QR code type, enter your data, and download in your preferred format — PNG, SVG, PDF, JPG, or WEBP. No registration required.

Yes. The generator supports links, Wi-Fi credentials, email, phone numbers, SMS, and plain text.

Yes. The generator encodes Unicode/UTF-8 directly, so Cyrillic, Latin text, numbers, and symbols work without transliteration.
